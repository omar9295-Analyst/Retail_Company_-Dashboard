**Retail Company Dashboard - Data Analysis Project**

**📌 Project Overview : **
This project explores how retail companies truly operate by analyzing operational and sales data to uncover different business dynamics. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of core KPIs, customer demographics, return rates, and space efficiency to drive actionable insights. 

**🛠️ Tools & Technologies : **

1-** Creating Database & Cleaning & Transforming :** Mysql
2-** Data Modeling & Visualization:** Power BI. 
3- **UI/UX Design:** Custom-designed layouts with interactive tooltips and drill-through features for deeper insights.  

**🗄️ Data Modeling & Methodology : **

The database utilizes a central schema design where a main fact table (Fact_Sales) connects to multiple supporting dimension tables (e.g., Product, Stores, Calendar) to facilitate easy and efficient data analysis.  Some of these dimension tables are also connected to other sub-tables to handle complex hierarchical data.  

**📊 Core KPIs:**


1- **Total Sales:** $1,764,546. 

2- **Total Profit:** $1,052,819.

3- **Profit Margin:** 59.67%. 

4- **Total Cost:** $711,728. 

5- **Net Quantity Sold:** 825K. 

6- **Average Order Value (AOV):** $6.54.  

7- **Total Transactions:** 269.72K. 

8- **Total Customers:** 10.281K. 

9- **Average Sales Per Store:** $73.52K across 24 stores.  

**💡 Key Business Insights:**

1- **Top Performers:** The "Hermanos" brand dominates the market, contributing 22.57% of sales ($23K), with "Hermanos Green Pepper" being the highest-selling individual product ($1,671). 

2- **Geographical Strengths:** Salem is the top-performing city generating $69K in sales, while Washington (WA) stands out as the best-performing state overall. 

3- **Customer Demographics:** The most significant customer base consists of Married Females and individuals within the $30K - $50K income bracket
(3.33K customers). Additionally, the "Golden Ages" group (76 - 116 years) represents the largest age segment with 5.97K customers. 

4- **Membership Behavior:** While the Golden membership yields the highest average basket size and order value, the Bronze membership drives the highest transaction volume and is the most profitable overall. 

5- **Returns & Quality:** The brand "King" has the highest return rate at 1.78%, and geographically, the city of Merida struggles with the highest return rate (1.17%). 

6- **Space Efficiency:** Store profitability does not solely depend on physical size; Store 13 (a Deluxe Supermarket) is the most profitable and highest-selling store ($68.68K) despite being highly space-efficient.  

7- **Seasonal Trends:** December peaks as the most important month across all sales trends.  

8- **Sustainability:** Although 56.0% of products are recyclable, a substantial 44.0% remain unrecyclable, presenting an area for improvement. 

**🚀 Strategic Recommendations**

1- **Focus on Top Performers:** Concentrate efforts and investments on the best-selling brands and cities to maximize revenue.  

2- **Address Quality Issues:** Investigate and fix quality problems specifically for brands with high return rates (e.g., King). 

3- **Inventory Management:** Increase stock levels for top-selling products like Hermanos Green Pepper.  

4- **Targeted Marketing:** Direct marketing efforts toward married customers and the medium-income bracket ($30K-$50K), as they have higher spending power.  

5- **Store Optimization:** Replicate the space-efficient layouts of top-performing locations (like Store 13) across other stores.

6- **Seasonal Staffing:** Monitor monthly sales patterns and proactively hire additional staff during peak periods, particularly in December. 

7- **Customer Loyalty:** Implement reward programs for high-spending customers to encourage repeat purchases. 

8- **Market Expansion:** Open new stores in strategic areas with currently low customer penetration.
